About Me

Currently at CERN's Radiation Protection Group, I assess and mitigate radiation hazards in high-energy physics environments. My work includes detailed FLUKA simulations for LHC operations and upgrades.

Past work at DESY and the University of Hamburg focused on calorimetry for the ILC, including semi-empirical hadronic shower modeling. I've contributed to fast simulation tools like GFLASH and Particle Flow Algorithms.

I also collaborated with the European Space Agency on muon tomography for planetary exploration.

Outside physics, I'm passionate about outdoor sport.

Highlights

FLUKA Radiation Simulations @ CERN

High-precision Monte Carlo analysis for LHC safety, activation studies, shielding, and dose estimation.

Muon Tomography for ESA

GEANT4-based simulations to detect lunar subsurface structures using cosmic ray muons.

CALICE-AHCAL Beam Tests

Calorimeter data collection at SPS for high-granularity hadron shower studies.
